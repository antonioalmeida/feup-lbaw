About Presto

Presto (from the latin Praesto, i.e., 'ready to help') is an online platform where people can ask questions and contribute with answers, developing a community of unique insights on a wide variety of topics.

In a time where knowledge is of the utmost importance, it can still be hard to find relevant information regarding certain topics. Presto provides a platform where anyone can ask or answer questions of just about anything. One can, then, quickly obtain insightful information from different perspectives on a particular subject.

A variety of other features are available aswell. Members can follow other users, topics or threads of their interest to always be updated. It is also possible to comment and rate all the content, so users can express their opinion on others' messages. Each user has a score derived from these ratings that tells their relevance and contribution to the community.
